About Us

Our commitment is to provide an outstanding service and ease for customers who wish to travel, Meet new peolpe, Obtain long lasting meomries and have a delightful time doing so. On a selection of our packages we provide a representative who will cater for all your needs, Our representatives are there for assisting you with any travelling around from excursions, Restaurants and to assist with any other bookings you wish to make while you are on your event. Our services range from companions for people travelling alone, Quick and easy packages without the stress of looking around for the best deals.

We have special packages for peculiar times of the year like christmas and valentines day.

We also provide a custom package deal which is designed by you, For you. We arrange Hen and stag partys where we cater to all of your hearts desires. If you and your friends wish to have a custom package and can't find the desired package and wish to book, We will create the package and make the package with or without having to meet other travellers.

Companions

Here at Norwich Travel Companions we understand that not everyone has some-one to travel with, So we provide contacts for you to browse before travelling. Our companions are happy to help with the travelling needs that you might have, We advise that you consider booking early. Our companions are not just for travelling purposes, company or just some-one to spend time with. Customers are to cover expensives which may not be included in your package price (e.g evening meal etc).
